Chaat Carnival Crescendo

Paneer Piquancy Paragon

Cubes of velvety paneer, marinated in a symphony of aromatic spices and herbs, then pan-seared to perfection, delivering a delectable burst of Indian cottage cheese goodness.

Mango Masala Mélange

A tropical interlude featuring ripe mango slices adorned with a vibrant masala medley, harmonizing the sweet and spicy notes in a refreshing and exotic prelude.

Dahi Kebab Duet

A harmonious duet of hung yogurt and mashed potatoes, spiced with delicate herbs, crusted to a golden brown, delivering a velvety and indulgent experience with every bite.

Middle Overs

Mystical Masala Melange

Dive into the enchanting world of spices with our masala-infused main course. Tender chicken simmered in a rich, aromatic blend of cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves, crowned with a garnish of fresh coriander.

Exotic Biryani Bliss

Embark on a culinary odyssey with our regal biryani, where fragrant basmati rice and marinated chicken or vegetables intertwine in a magical fusion. Garnished with golden fried onions and mint, it's a taste of paradise.

Charm of Chana Masala

Unveil the charm of chickpeas with our enchanting Chana Masala. Slow-cooked chickpeas in a tantalizing blend of tomatoes, ginger, and aromatic spices, creating a melody of flavors for the discerning palate.

Divine Dhal Delicacy

Death Overs

Chenapoda Concoction

Unveil the magic of Chenapoda, a masterpiece hailing from the kitchens of Odisha. This caramelized wonder boasts a harmonious blend of freshly grated paneer, semolina, aromatic cardamom, and a whisper of saffron. Baked to perfection, it emerges as a golden-brown marvel, offering a symphony of textures and tastes.

Rasmalai Reverie

Dive into the delicate world of Rasmalai, where flattened chhena discs luxuriate in a bath of creamy, cardamom-kissed milk. Each bite unravels a captivating dance of sweetness and silkiness, leaving an indelible mark on your palate.

Kesar Pista Paragon

Savor the regality of Kesar Pista, a celestial blend of saffron-laced milk reduction and the crunch of finely chopped pistachios. Embellished with edible silver, this dessert stands as a testament to the opulence of Indian sweet mastery.

Phirni Fantasy

Immerse yourself in the allure of Phirni, a velvety rice pudding kissed by the essence of rose water and adorned with slivered almonds. This dessert captivates with its creamy allure, creating a dreamy symphony on your palate.
